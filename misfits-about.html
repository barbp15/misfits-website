Hello and Welcome to Misfits RC Drag Club!

We are a new, family oriented radio controlled drag racing club open to all ages and abilities. We get together once a month to practice and hone our racing skills. No money or trophies involved at this point, but possibly in the future. The focus is on GOOD CLEAN FUN. We race any and all makes of RC cars and trucks.


Dates and times of meets will be posted on our website. The meeting place is the Neidig Elementary School parking lot, Penrose Street, Quakertown, PA.
